Edentu Privacy Policy

Personal Info

At Edentu, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your personal information. This Data Protection Policy outlines our collection and use of your data. We take appropriate measures to safeguard your personal information, and you may contact us at any time to inquire about the personal information we have stored about you.

How We Use Your Info

We will only process your personal information in accordance with your consent and for the fulfillment of our commitment to you. Edentu and our authorized personal data assistants are the only ones who handle and use your personal information. We do not share any of our customers' personal data with other companies or authorities unless required by law, regulation, or official decision.

How We Protect Your Info

Edentu is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is kept strictly confidential. We utilize the highest levels of security and safety to maintain the confidentiality of your personal information. We will not disclose or sell any of your personal information, including your name, address, age, or contact details, to any third party without your express permission.
